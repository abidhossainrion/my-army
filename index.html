Bangladesh Military Academy

The Bangladesh Military Academy (BMA) is an esteemed institution that plays a crucial role in shaping the future leaders of the Bangladesh Armed Forces


We work for protect Bangladesh

BMA is situated in Bhatiary, near Chittagong. The academy's location provides a conducive environment for military training, with access to diverse terrains that aid in preparing officers for various operational challenges.


We Protect the sky

The academy offers a comprehensive curriculum that includes academic, military, and physical training. Cadets undergo rigorous training programs to prepare them for the multifaceted responsibilities they will shoulder as officers in the armed forces.


We have the best snipper

Army commando course and counter-insurgency course began in 1980, and in 1982 the school was reorganized as the Special Warfare Wing of the Bangladesh Army School of Infantry and Tactics (SI&T). The Bangladesh Army Sniper Team placed 7th in the 2017 International Army Games in Kazakhstan

Military Objectives


  • Objective 1

    To instill in the trainees the qualities of a leader imbibed with the national, societal and religious values, military tradition and dedication to a lifetime service to the nation.

  • Objective 2

    To train each Trainee Officer and Officer Cadet as a competent Infantry Platoon Commander committed to professionalism.

  • Objective 3

    To educate each Officer Cadet as a knowledgeable university graduate with inspiration to pursue higher education.


History Of BMA

Post-Independence Vision

After the independence of Bangladesh, the Father of the Nation Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman envisioned the necessity to establish a military training institution to train and build up a professional, dedicated and patriotic army officers who will safeguard the sovereignty, integrity and freedom of our nation.

Establishment of BMA

To materialize his noble vision Bangladesh Military Academy (BMA) was established and inaugurated at Cumilla Cantonment in 1974...

Training and Curriculum

BMA offers military and academic training emphasizing on individual character building. The academy primarily trains men and women to be commissioned into the Bangladesh Army...

Paradigm Shift in Training

From January 2015, a paradigm shift in the training of officer cadets encompassing three cardinal training modules such as character, academic and military have been redefined..
